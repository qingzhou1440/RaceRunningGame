🎯How to Play

Getting started with Paper Boy Race Running Game is simple! Use the arrow keys or touch controls to move your character left and right. Jump over obstacles by pressing the spacebar or tapping the screen.

Your goal is to collect as many newspapers as possible while avoiding cars, pedestrians, and other street obstacles. The longer you survive, the faster the game becomes, testing your reflexes and decision-making skills.

Keep an eye out for power-ups that can help you along the way, and try to beat your high score with each playthrough!
